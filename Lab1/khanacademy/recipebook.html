Recipe #1

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: hot
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs 4
Tomato 1
Butter 50g
Salt & Pepper 1 teaspoon

Step 1: Add butter on pan and light a fire on the stove.

Step 2: Drop eggs on pan.

Step 3: Slice tomato and add it to eggs.

Step 4: Add salt and pepper on pan.

Step 4: Eat!
